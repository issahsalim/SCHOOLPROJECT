How to Report an Absence Effectively

Follow these guidelines to ensure your absence report is processed smoothly:

  • Clearly state the reason for your child's absence
  • Upload your child's photo to help school staff identify them (optional)
  • Ensure all information provided is accurate and complete
  • Submit the form at least 24 hours before the absence when possible
